PDH quantum shot noise is a fundamental noise floor for laser measurements.

Pound-Drever-Hall Shot Noise $\left[ \dfrac{ \text{W}^2 }{ \text{Hz} } \right]$

The equation for quantum PDH shot noise is \[ S(f) = 2 \hbar \omega_0 P_0 \left[J_0(\Gamma)^2 (1-\eta) +3 J_1(\Gamma)^2\right] \] where $\hbar$ is the reduced Planck's constant, $\omega_0$ is the carrier laser frequency, $P_0$ is the power incident on the photodetector, $J_0$ and $J_1$ are Bessel functions of the first kind, $\Gamma$ is the modulation depth, and $\eta$ is your Fabry-Perot cavity visibility.
